Open-archaeo is a list of open source tools and resources designed by and for archaeologists, with archaeological purposes in mind. In addition to serving as an index pointing to the resources themselves, open-archaeo highlights the magnificient people, organizations and initiatives who produce them. Additions and updates may be tracked using the open-archaeo RSS feed.

Contributing to the list

If you are aware of relevant software or resources that are not already on the list, please use the webform to suggest them as additional items and include as much detail as possible. Please also get in touch to request that changes be made to existing items. All data will be reviewed to ensure its accuracy and to avoid duplicate records.

Information about maintainers is reviewed and updated manually to ensure that it is accurate and to ensure that maintainers have control over what personal information is made available on the web. Only maintainers' names will be listed by default, but other information such as professional affiliations, contact details and social media profiles may be added on an opt-in basis. Please contact me using the webform to request that personal information be added, updated or removed.
